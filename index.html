Creative Corner IND Detail About

Art has been always considered as the image of any country or place as it shows shades of cultural, social and economical aspects of that country or place and its people. Art has been a specialty of our nation as India has been blessed with so many renowned artists and craftworkers in the past. Art has always been used, in our country, for imprinting the social, cultural and moral values in the younger generations. Art cannot be defined in a single word or single work. Any painting made by someone using his imaginative ideas and technical skills is also an artwork, any activity that is innovative and has a sense of beauty and is related to our culture is also an artwork. Like these, there are so many forms of art that we encounter in our daily lives. Some of them get recognized but most of them are left aside irrespective of the fact that they have the potential for being recognized. Only the artist knows how much important that art is for him and how many emotions he has gone through before he created that art of his. So, Creative Corner IND has been created for just working on that aspect and to provide the actual recognition that it deserves.
